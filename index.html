1.1. This is a placement agreement (‘Agreement’) between you and Build Launch.

1.2. The terms and conditions of your placement are set out in the Subcontractor Agreement, and this Placement Agreement.

 

2. Host Company

2.1. Your Host Company is identified in Item 9 of Schedule 1 (‘’).

 

3. Location

3.1. You will be required to perform your duties primarily in the time zone of the State or Territory identified in Item 11 & 12 of Schedule 1 of Schedule 1, or elsewhere as reasonably directed by the Host.

 

4. Position

4.1.    The position in which you will be placed is stated in Item 1 of Schedule 1.

4.2.   Your duties and responsibilities are outlined in the Position Description in Annexure A.

4.3.   You will be required to perform any other duties the host may assign to you, having regard to your skills, training, and experience.

4.4   You will report to the person named in Item 2 of Schedule 1.

4.5   If your position changes for any reason, the other terms of this Agreement will remain in force unless expressly varied by the parties in writing, to the extent permitted by law.

 

5. Commencement

5.1   Your placement will commence on stated in Item 3 of Schedule 1.

 

6. Obligations

6.1    You are required to:

    (a) Observe & follow Build Launch’s and the Host’s policies and procedures

    (b) Perform your duties to the best of your ability,

    (c) Undertake work with due care, skill, and diligence,

    (d) Faithfully and honestly serve the Host, Build Launch and its clients by acting in their best interests,

    (e) Using your best endeavours to promote and protect the interests of the Host & Build Launch,

    (f) Using your best endeavours to promote and protect the interests of the Host & Build Launch,

    (g) Promptly notify the Host and Build Launch of any damage to company property in your possession,

    (h) Wear a correct and tidy uniform daily and maintain a high level of presentation which is expected.
